Margherita Pizza

Description

The classic Italian pizza. It represents the colors of the Italian flag: red (tomato), white (mozzarella), and green (basil). Simple, fresh, and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 pre-made pizza dough ball
  • 1/2 cup tomato pizza sauce
  • 200g fresh mozzarella cheese,sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • A pinch of Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 250°C.
  2. Stretch the dough into a thin circle on a floured surface.
  3. Spread sauce evenly and top with mozzarella slices.
  4. Bake for 8-10 minutes until the crust is golden.
  5. Top with fresh basil and a drizzle of oil before serving.
