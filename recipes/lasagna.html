Lasagna

Description

This world-famous lasagna recipe features a hearty meat sauce and three types of cheese.

It takes some time to prepare, but it is the ultimate comfort food for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 1/2 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. In a large pot, cook sausage, beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until browned.
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and water.
  3. Layer the noodles, meat mixture, and cheese in a baking dish.
  4. Bake at 190°C (375°F) for about 50 minutes.
