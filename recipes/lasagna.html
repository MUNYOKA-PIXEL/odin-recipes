Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian comfort food consisting of layers of flat pasta, savory meat sauce, and melted cheese.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or sausage
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Marinara sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles until al dente.
  2. Brown the meat and mix with sauce.
  3. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheese in a baking dish.
  4. Bake until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
