Beef Tacos

Description

A quick and vibrant Mexican-inspired dish. These tacos use seasoned ground beef, fresh vegetables, and crunchy shells for the perfect weeknight meal.

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 tablespoon taco seasoning (cumin, chili powder, garlic powder)
  • 12 hard taco shells
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Shredded cheddar cheese
  • Sour cream

Steps

  1. Brown the ground beef in a skillet over medium heat until no longer pink.
  2. Drain the excess fat and stir in the taco seasoning and a splash of water.
  3. Warm the taco shells in the oven at 180°C for 5 minutes.
  4. Spoon the beef into the shells and top with lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, and sour cream.
